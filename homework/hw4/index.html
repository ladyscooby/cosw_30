Who We Are

Beach Saviors is an environmental nonprofit dedicated to making the coastal waters and watersheds of Greater Los Angeles safe, healthy and clean. To fulfill our mission, we use science, education, community action, and advocacy.

What We Do

For 34 years, we’ve been the Southland’s strongest advocate for the ocean. But a healthy Bay requires a healthy L.A. Envisioning thriving oceans, healthy watersheds and smart water, we’re working toward a greener, bluer Los Angeles County for everyone.
